About Sambek Roofing

Building trust one roof at a time

Founded in 2010, Sambek Roofing has grown from a small family business to one of the region's most trusted roofing contractors. Our founder, Sam, started with a simple philosophy: treat every home as if it were your own.

Today, we maintain that same commitment to quality and customer service. We specialize in both residential and commercial roofing, offering comprehensive solutions from minor repairs to complete roof replacements.
